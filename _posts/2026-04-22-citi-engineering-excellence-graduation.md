Delighted to share that I have completed Citi's highest-level **Engineering Excellence Talent Program** — a milestone that would not have been possible without an extraordinary network of mentors and colleagues across the firm.

The programme deepened my work on Generative AI projects and Open Source AI initiatives, in particular the [FINOS AI Governance Framework](https://github.com/finos/ai-governance-framework) — an effort I am proud to maintain alongside a remarkable cross-institutional community.

What I carry forward from this experience goes beyond technical skill. In the GenAI era, **sound judgement and delivering sustainable solutions that provide a net positive impact** matter as much as any model architecture or platform choice. Perhaps more.

My thanks to Anthony Warden, Ramkumar Ayyadurai, Daniel Jepp, Miriam Silver, and the many colleagues across multiple regions who gave generously of their time, guidance, and trust.
