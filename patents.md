## Lead Patents — Regulatory Compliance

<div class="pub-entry">
  <div class="pub-venue">Citibank, N.A. · US12111754B1 · Granted October 8, 2024</div>
  <div class="pub-title"><a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12111754B1/en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dynamically Validating AI Applications for Compliance</a></div>
  <div class="pub-abstract">A system that evaluates the compliance of an AI application with predefined guidelines. The technology obtains a set of guidelines defining operation boundaries of the AI application and constructs test cases associated with each guideline — each comprising a prompt, an expected outcome, and an expected explanation. It supplies the prompts to the AI application, receives case-specific outcomes and explanations, and compares them with expected results. A compliance indicator is generated based on the evaluation, indicating the degree of compliance of the AI application with the guidelines.</div>

<div class="pub-entry">
  <div class="pub-venue">Citibank, N.A. · US12361335B1 · Granted July 15, 2025</div>
  <div class="pub-title"><a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12361335B1/en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Validating Vector Constraints of Outputs Generated by Machine Learning Models</a></div>
  <div class="pub-abstract">A system that evaluates the compliance of an AI application with predefined vector constraints. Multiple specialised models — trained to identify specific types of non-compliance — analyse the representation of attributes within AI-generated responses, identify vector representations of alphanumeric characters by assessing proximate locations, frequency, and associations, and determine indicators of vector alignment by measuring differences in the direction or magnitude of vector representations against predetermined characters.</div>

## Co-Patents — AI Applications

  <div class="pub-title"><a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12299140B2/en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dynamic Multi-Model Monitoring and Validation for Artificial Intelligence Models</a></div>
  <div class="pub-abstract">A multi-model superstructure that dynamically routes the artifacts of a first set of models to one or more models of a second set for evaluation against assessment metrics. <div class="pub-entry">

  <div class="pub-title"><a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12346820B1/en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Identifying and Remediating Gaps in Artificial Intelligence Use Cases Using a Generative AI Model</a></div>
  <div class="pub-abstract">Systems that receive regulatory definitions establishing boundaries for expected AI use cases, paired with operational data. An initial AI model constructs observed use cases from the operational data; a second AI model correlates regulatory definitions and use case attributes to an appropriate risk classification. The system identifies compliance shortfalls within each observed use case and produces recommended remediation actions, enabling continuous alignment of AI deployments to regulatory requirements.</div>

<div class="pub-entry">

  <div class="pub-title"><a href="https://patents.google.com/patent/US12450494B1/en" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Validating Autonomous Artificial Intelligence (AI) Agents Using Generative AI</a></div>
  <div class="pub-abstract">A proactive validation layer that continuously monitors and evaluates the actions of autonomous agents using generative AI models. The system receives constraints and operational data defining agent parameters, dynamically evaluates agent behaviours for compliance with predefined boundaries and regulatory standards, maps agents to risk categories, generates expected actions, identifies gaps between expected and actual outputs, and modifies proposed actions through AI-driven analysis — enabling agents to execute refined actions while preventing unauthorised behaviour and maintaining audit trails for transparency.</div>
